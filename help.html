Crashery is fun little project. It's a web app that makes sense of your Android crash reports.

What will it do?

Crashery will do very little, while still being crazy useful. Just enter your ACRA spreadsheet URL in the textbox below to see for yourself.

How do I use it?

First, make sure your ACRA spreadsheet is shared Publicly in Google Documents. If you don't know what ACRA is, start here.

It's also important that your spreadsheet columns mirror mine, which is ACRA's default. They'll need to be in the exact-same order as the sample spreadsheet listed below.
